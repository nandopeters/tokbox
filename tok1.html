iTel Companies, Inc. (iTel™) is an early stage private healthcare IT company (Delaware C-Corp) located in Scottsdale, Arizona, that links providers and patients with proprietary technologies through a secure encrypted video-conferencing software capability via its principal product, Secure-Health™ VIDEO, allowing the patient to have a secure appointment from their home or office, or while traveling. It is unique in that the technology brings consistent, real-time medical-grade diagnostic quality video-conferencing (VTC) with the added Region of Interest (ROI) to patients & clinicians, even via wireless, or an inexpensive single dial-up phone (POTS) line.
